This is the beginning of the end for those candidates who have not made clear that they support fundamental reform and an end to moneyed corruption. We want you to help us get the word out to candidates: back reform legislation and inoculate yourself from the Mayday PAC. Otherwise, continue at your own risk. ### We want you to call or email your Representative and Senators, (or challengers to their positions), and help us keep track of who has been called, and how often, using the form below. {:.no_toc} This can be done in four easy steps: 1. Go to [http://www.reform.to](http://www.reform.to) 2. Type in your address or zip to search for your representative or candidate. 3. Do they support reform? If YES, send them a thank you! If NO, send them an email, call them, or show up and demand an answer. 4. Tell us who you contacted below so we can keep track of responses. This will also help us figure out who would stand for reform, but who we may have overlooked, as well as those candidates and incumbents who have changed their minds on this issue. **Deadline:** Candidates have until 5 PM EST next Tuesday, August 5, to inoculate themselves. That means we have one week to urge candidates to do the right thing.
